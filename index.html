Akan names

Traditions in Ghana as far as naming of both male and female focuses on mainly days of the week.

Each day of the week represents an akan name as shown below.

Male

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednessday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

Female

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednessday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
